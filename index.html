Help Center & FAQ

Everything you need to know about using OmniCart, troubleshooting errors, and optimizing your shopping experience.

General Questions & Troubleshooting

Which stores are supported?

Our Universal Scraper Engine works natively with over 10,000 e-commerce sites, including Amazon, Walmart, Target, eBay, AliExpress, Best Buy, Newegg, Home Depot, IKEA, Macy's, Costco, Nike, Apple, Shein, ASOS, and thousands of independent Shopify/BigCommerce/Wix stores. If it has a price tag, we can usually scrape it.

Why did an item say "Missing Details" or "Anti-Bot Protections"?

Some major retailers employ aggressive anti-bot software that actively block web scrapers. If our engine gets completely blocked, we will show an error. If we manage to bypass part of it but miss a piece of data, it will return a partial result and ask you to quickly fill in the title and price manually.

Can I actually purchase items through OmniCart?

No. OmniCart is a universal planning and organization tool. When you click "Checkout" or click on a product link, we redirect you securely back to the original retailer's website where you complete the actual purchase natively.

Why aren't my cart items syncing across devices?

Check your internet connection. OmniCart automatically pushes changes to our secure MongoDB cloud servers every time you add, remove, or check off an item. If you have multiple tabs open, try refreshing to force a sync pull from the database.

How do Shared Workspaces function?

By clicking the "Share" button at the top of a workspace, you can toggle "Public Access". This generates a unique link. Anyone with that link can view your workspace, see the items, and click the links to buy them. They cannot delete or modify items.

I forgot my password and the reset link isn't arriving?

If you created your account using the "Continue with Google" button, you do not have a standard password. You must click the Google button to log back in. If you wish to set a password for a Google-linked account, the reset link *should* arrive, but check your spam folder.

Troubleshooting: Pop-ups Blocked

Why isn't "Batch Checkout" / "Checkout Unbought" working?

To save you time, OmniCart tries to open all your unbought items in new tabs simultaneously. Modern browsers classify opening multiple tabs at once as a "Pop-up" and block it by default to protect you from spam.

How to allow OmniCart pop-ups:
  • Google Chrome: Look for the "Pop-up blocked" icon in the far right of your address bar. Click it, select "Always allow pop-ups and redirects from omni-cart.org", and click Done.
  • Safari (Mac): Go to Safari > Settings > Websites > Pop-up Windows. Find omni-cart.org in the list and change the dropdown to "Allow".
  • Firefox: A yellow banner will appear below the address bar. Click "Options" and select "Allow pop-ups for omni-cart.org".
  • Edge: Click the "Pop-up blocked" icon in the address bar and select "Always allow".
